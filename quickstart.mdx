## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a demo">
    [Schedule a 30-minute onboarding call](https://cal.com/akiratong/30min?overlayCalendar=true) with our team. We'll walk through your stack and connect your tools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your tools">
    During onboarding, we'll integrate Arga with your context sources — GitHub, Slack, Jira, Sentry, Grafana, and others. See the full list of [supported integrations](/integrations).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first validation">
    Start with the workflow that matches your goal. Use **Test my app** for a one-off URL check, **Automatic GitHub validation** for PR and branch checks, **Sandbox testing** for isolated end-to-end flows, or **Scan my app** for adversarial exploration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens during onboarding

We'll need to understand your current tooling so we can configure Arga for your stack:

1. **Observability and issue tracking** — Which tools do you use? (Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana, Sentry, Jira, Linear, etc.)
2. **Logging and monitoring** — Which log aggregation tools do you use? (Loki, ELK, Splunk, Honeycomb, etc.)
3. **Features of interest** — What do you want to use Arga for? (PR validation, agent testing, red-teaming, etc.)

See the full [integrations page](/integrations) for details on what we support.
